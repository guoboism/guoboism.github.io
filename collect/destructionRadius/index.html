DESTRUCTION DARIUS

Posted on Dec 31, 2015
#indiesvsgamers

In this game, you drop bombs to clear a way to reach the target place.

But not only the explosion will kill you, the after-effect of the blocks are dangerous too!

The concrete block will break into pieces that would fall around;

the wooden blocks contains fragements bouncing fast between walls;

and there are boxes that would spawn more bombs when exploded.

The game combines puzzle (how to bomb) and action (evade dangerouse stuff).

Bomb is always a duo-effect (both good and bad) action in games, and the introduction of various blocks and their after effects multiplies the possibilities dramatic consequences.
